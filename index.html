Using Internet Explorer? Need help installing?

  • Internet Explorer
    1. Make sure your "Links Toolbar" or "Favorites" is visible. If it is not, right click on any part of the menu bar and make sure "Links" or "Favorites Bar" is checked.
    2. Right click 5 Second Bookmarklet and click "Add to Favorites."
    3. Be sure to save the favorite in your "Links" folder so that it appears in your Links Toolbar.
  • Firefox
    1. Make sure the "Bookmarks Toolbar" is visible. If it is not, go to menu View > Toolbars.
    2. Drag the 5 Second Films button up to your Bookmarks Toolbar.
  • Safari
    1. Make sure the "Bookmarks Bar" is visible. If it is not, go to the menu View > Show Bookmarks Bar.
    2. Drag the 5 Second Films button up to your Bookmarks Toolbar.
